Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MediaGet handles information when you use our Instagram media downloader, TikTok video downloader, and YouTube video downloader services.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to process your download request. When you submit a URL, that URL is used to fetch the requested media and is stored temporarily in order to complete the download.

We do not require registration, an email address, or any personal identification to use our service.

How We Use Information

Submitted URLs and associated data are used only to process download requests, maintain service quality, and prevent abuse. We do not use your information for marketing or profiling.

  • Process download requests
  • Fix issues and improve the service
  • Protect against abuse and spam

Data Retention

Downloaded content is stored temporarily and removed automatically after a short period. We do not keep downloads or submitted URLs longer than necessary to complete the request.

Cookies and Local Storage

We may use essential cookies to support the website's basic functions. We do not use third-party tracking, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies on our downloader pages.

Third-Party Services

Some features rely on external platforms to retrieve public media. For example, our YouTube downloader interacts with YouTube to fetch preview data. We do not control how those platforms handle your data, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

Security

We use HTTPS to protect data in transit and take reasonable measures to secure our systems. However, no internet service can guarantee complete security.

Children's Privacy

MediaGet is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has shared personal data with us, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of MediaGet after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us. We are committed to protecting your privacy while delivering a useful download experience.
